# Focus Adventure

### A Free and Open-Source Adventure Built for Kids with ADHD

**Focus Adventure is, and is intended to remain, free for children and families.**

Focus Adventure was created with a simple mission:

> **To make focus-building and cognitive activities fun, encouraging, accessible, and freely available to children with ADHD.**

Children with ADHD can face challenges with attention, working memory, impulse control, task switching, motivation, and staying engaged with repetitive activities.

Focus Adventure aims to turn activities that exercise these skills into something children can actually enjoy — **games, challenges, quests, rewards, and adventures.**

Instead of making practice feel like homework, we want it to feel like play.

## Our Promise: Free for Kids

We believe that a child's ability to access helpful educational and focus-building activities should not depend on whether their family can afford another subscription.

Our goal is simple:

**Keep the core Focus Adventure experience free for children and families.**

No child should be excluded from the core experience because their family cannot afford to pay.

As the project grows, we want this principle to remain at the heart of every decision we make.

## Why Focus Adventure Exists

Focus Adventure is being built especially with **children with ADHD in mind**.

The platform combines short, engaging activities designed around skills such as:

* Attention and sustained focus
* Working memory
* Impulse control and inhibition
* Reaction and response control
* Cognitive flexibility
* Task switching
* Visual attention
* Pattern recognition
* Problem solving

But Focus Adventure is not about telling a child that something is "wrong" with them.

It is about creating an environment where children can:

**Play. Learn. Practice. Progress. And feel proud of what they accomplish.**

The experience is designed to encourage children through achievable challenges, positive feedback, progression, and playful interaction.

## Important Disclaimer

Focus Adventure is an educational and supportive application designed with children with ADHD in mind.

It is **not a medical device** and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent ADHD or any other medical or psychological condition.

The games, exercises, and activities in Focus Adventure should not be considered a replacement for professional medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, medication, educational support, or treatment.

Parents and caregivers should consult qualified healthcare or educational professionals regarding a child's individual needs.

The project aims to complement a child's broader support system by providing an accessible, engaging, and enjoyable environment for practicing focus-related and cognitive skills.
