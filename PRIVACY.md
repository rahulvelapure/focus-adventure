# Privacy Policy

## Our Commitment to Privacy

Focus Adventure is an open-source application designed with children, including children with ADHD, in mind.

Protecting the privacy and safety of children and families is a core principle of this project.

Our goal is to minimize the collection of personal information and design Focus Adventure using privacy-first principles.

## Information Collection

The open-source Focus Adventure application should not intentionally collect personal information from children unless such functionality is explicitly introduced, documented, reviewed, and implemented with appropriate parental consent and legal safeguards.

Where possible, game progress, preferences, settings, and similar information should be stored locally on the user's device rather than transmitted to external services.

## Children's Privacy

Focus Adventure is intended to be usable by children and families.

Features involving children's personal information require additional privacy and safety review before implementation.

Contributors must not introduce unnecessary collection of information such as:

- Full names
- Email addresses
- Precise location
- Phone numbers
- Home addresses
- School information
- Photos or videos identifying children
- Behavioral advertising profiles
- Device fingerprinting
- Unnecessary tracking identifiers

Any future feature requiring personal information must follow applicable children's privacy and data-protection requirements.

## Analytics and Tracking

Focus Adventure aims to minimize tracking.

Advertising trackers, behavioral profiling, device fingerprinting, or unnecessary third-party analytics should not be introduced without explicit project review.

Any analytics introduced in the future must be clearly documented and designed with children's privacy as a primary consideration.

## Advertising

The core Focus Adventure experience is intended to remain free and accessible to children and families.

The project should not use manipulative advertising practices or behavioral advertising targeted at children.

Any future funding or sustainability model must be evaluated carefully against the project's child-safety, privacy, accessibility, and free-access principles.

## Local Storage

Focus Adventure may use browser or device storage for functionality such as:

- Game progress
- Scores
- Preferences
- Settings
- Difficulty
- Achievements or progression

Information stored locally generally remains on the user's device unless functionality is explicitly implemented to synchronize it elsewhere.

Users may be able to remove locally stored information by clearing application or browser data.

## Third-Party Services

If Focus Adventure uses third-party services in the future, those services must be reviewed for privacy and security implications.

The project should avoid sharing children's information with third parties unless strictly necessary, appropriately disclosed, legally compliant, and protected by suitable safeguards.

## Open-Source Contributions

Contributors must not commit personal information, credentials, private user data, API keys, passwords, access tokens, or other sensitive information to the public repository.

Features involving:

- User accounts
- Cloud synchronization
- Analytics
- Authentication
- Social features
- Online communication
- Artificial intelligence services
- External APIs
- Children's personal information

should receive privacy and security review before implementation.

## Parental and Caregiver Involvement

Where appropriate, Focus Adventure should favor designs that give parents and caregivers appropriate visibility and control over features involving children's information.

Features requiring accounts, online communication, data sharing, or cloud storage should be designed with appropriate parental consent and control mechanisms where required.

## Medical and Health Information

Focus Adventure is not intended to collect medical records or provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

Contributors should avoid creating functionality that unnecessarily collects sensitive medical or health information.

Focus Adventure is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, or treatment.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may evolve as Focus Adventure develops.

Material changes involving data collection, accounts, analytics, cloud services, or third-party integrations should be clearly documented.

## Contact

Privacy questions or concerns may be raised through the appropriate contact or reporting channels provided by the Focus Adventure project.

Security vulnerabilities should be reported privately according to the process described in `SECURITY.md`.
